Tree Atlas

A guide to navigating and understanding your atlas

Getting Around

The gallery is arranged as a table. Each column is a different subject. Each row shows a different characteristic of that subject. One photo fills the screen at a time.

Swipe left / right
Move between subjects. Each swipe snaps to the next column. On desktop, use the arrow buttons or left/right arrow keys.
Scroll up / down
Move between rows within a column. Each scroll snaps to the next characteristic. The small dots on the right edge track your vertical position.

The dots along the bottom edge show which column you're in. The dots along the right edge show which row. Together they tell you exactly where you are in the grid.

Viewing Photos Up Close

Any photo can be enlarged for a closer look.

Pinch
Two finger pinch zooms in or out. On desktop, use the scroll wheel.
Drag
When zoomed in, drag with one finger to pan around the photo and explore details. Swipe or scroll to other columns or rows outside the photo.

What the Rows Mean

Each column follows the same structure from top to bottom, so you can compare the same characteristic across different subjects by swiping left or right.
